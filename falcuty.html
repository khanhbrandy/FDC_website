MEET OUR FALCUTY

Imagine being mentored by the great minds you most respect. Imagine learning from most renowned industry experts. Imagine participating in intensive seminars led by faculty on the forefront of research and scholarship.

In Data Science in Economics and Business program, you will be surrounded by lecturers and mentors who are leading their fields. They bring real-world experience on the cutting edge of thought and creativity to the classroom, fostering a dynamic and demanding academic environment. Our lecturers encourage students to think broadly, analytically, and critically.

Our faculty create active partnerships with students. They will consult with you about your career path, invite you to join their own projects, or help you design your own.
